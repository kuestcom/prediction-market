---
title: Account Security
description: Practical steps to keep your account and wallet safe
---


## Security checklist

- Enable <strong>Two-Factor Authentication</strong> in <strong>/settings/account</strong>.
- Verify the site URL before signing messages.
- Never share seed phrase, private key, or backup codes.
- Revoke unused wallet connections periodically.

## Account deletion

Use <strong>/settings/account</strong> only when you want to permanently delete your account.
The confirmation dialog requires typing <strong>DELETE</strong>, then removes your account data and signs you out.

## Signature safety

Before approving any signature or transaction:

- verify network/account
- review destination/action
- confirm amount/token details

If anything looks unexpected, reject and contact support.
