## Where to manage it

Open <strong>/settings/affiliate</strong> to view:

- your referral link
- commission rate
- referrals and active traders
- referred volume and earned fees
- the <strong>Affiliate Commissions</strong> card to claim your affiliate fees

## How commission is calculated

- Current taker fee: <strong><TradingFeeDisplay /></strong>
- Your affiliate share of that fee: <strong><AffiliateShareDisplay /></strong>

<Callout type="info" title="Execution basis">
  Commission is taken from operator fees at order execution time, not directly from raw volume.
</Callout>

## Claiming fees

Affiliate fees can be claimed from the <strong>Affiliate Commissions</strong> card in <strong>/settings/affiliate</strong>.

## Payout timing

Affiliate earnings update as referred trades settle and can be tracked directly in the affiliate dashboard.
