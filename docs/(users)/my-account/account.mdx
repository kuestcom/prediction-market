## Account settings

Open <strong>/settings/account</strong> to manage account-level security and deletion settings.

The Account page contains:

- <strong>Two-Factor Authentication</strong>: add or remove authenticator-app verification
- <strong>Delete account</strong>: permanently remove your account after confirmation

## Two-factor authentication

2FA uses a 6-digit code from an authenticator app such as Google Authenticator or Authy.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open Account

    Go to <strong>Settings → Account</strong>.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Start setup

    Click <strong>Enable 2FA</strong>.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Add the authenticator entry

    Scan the QR code, open the authenticator link on mobile, or copy the setup secret into your authenticator app.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Verify the code

    Enter the 6-digit code shown by your authenticator app. You can also choose to trust the current device for 30 days after activation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After 2FA is enabled, sign-in may redirect you to <strong>/2fa</strong> to enter your authenticator code before continuing.

## Delete account

Account deletion is permanent. The confirmation dialog asks you to type <strong>DELETE</strong> before the final action is enabled.

Deleting your account removes your platform account, sessions, linked wallet records, 2FA setup, order records, and referral relationships. You are signed out after deletion completes.

<Callout type="warning" title="Permanent action">
  Account deletion cannot be undone. Export any records you need before deleting your account.
</Callout>
