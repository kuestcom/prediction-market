## Notification Channels

You can configure how you receive alerts:

- **Email**
  - Market resolutions → Get notified when a market you hold positions in is resolved

- **In-app**
  - Order fills → Alerts when your orders are filled
  - Hide small fills (&lt;1 share) → Option to ignore micro fills
  - Resolutions → In-app notification when markets resolve

---

## How to Configure

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open notification settings">

    Navigate to <strong>My Account → Notifications</strong> to adjust how
    you’re alerted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle channels">

    Enable or disable email and in-app switches for fills, resolutions, and
    other alerts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your choices">

    Changes apply instantly—no extra confirmation needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
