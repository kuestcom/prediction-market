<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What fees do I pay when I trade?">
    Each trade includes a <strong>1% protocol fee</strong> plus the platform’s
    trading fee (currently 1%). Part of the platform fee can be shared with
    affiliates, and the exact amount appears before you confirm any order.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I claim my winnings?">
    Visit <strong>My Account → Portfolio</strong> and press the
    <strong>Claim</strong> button next to markets that resolved in your favor.
    Payouts hit your balance right away.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long do deposits take?">
    Crypto deposits usually confirm within minutes, depending on network
    congestion. Fiat on-ramps can take several hours or up to two business days
    based on your provider.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I disagree with a market resolution?">
    Use the <strong>Dispute</strong> button on the market page during the listed
    dispute window. You’ll stake a small fee and must supply verifiable
    evidence; valid disputes receive the stake back.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are there withdrawal limits?">
    Crypto withdrawals are generally unlimited. Fiat withdrawals may have
    provider-specific limits, and all requests are processed as quickly as
    possible.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
